Majestic Fury
The Campaign
Long ago, our great land was once ruled by explosive and capricious dragons. No creature in Miscrule could match their prowess, for the dragon’s scales were unpenetrable by common weapons and many suffered under the wrath of these hedonistic creatures as they tore the land asunder, lawless in their power. At an attempt for peace, 6 of the most powerful warriors in Miscrule formed an ancient and soul-wrenching seal that brought all of the dragons under the control of one, one who could mediate them and end their reign of tyranny. We call that one the Dragonrider. The end of the era of the dragons brought about the First Rule, under King Arcanthus of Memory. Glory to his name. He and his 5 hierarchs divided the land into territories that would be ruled autonomously by the 19 races of Miscrule. Without the Dragonrider, our land would once again be plagued by these fowl beasts. As long as the Dragonrider remains, we are safe. All hail the Dragonrider!

Affinity
This world is all about the creatures of this land. When two creatures bond a special connection is created. That connection is called an AFFINITY, a powerful bond that allows each of you to draw strength from one another. When bonding, you have access to the creature’s physical and spiritual powers. You can summon it to fight or use its talents for other purposes as well. The stronger the bond, the stronger you both become. However, bonding is a two-way relationship. If you and your creature have an unstable relationship it will have a dire effect on your sanity and powers. You can have more than one affinity, however the more creatures you bond with, the weaker your bonds become since your loyalty becomes divided. Affinity can be used for power, for enlightenment, or for love. You can only bond with an unbonded creature. So whoever you choose to have an affinity towards, remember to choose well.

You can go through the entire campaign without bonding with a creature, however you will be significantly less cool and less likely to do cool things. You may have an affinity towards more than one creature, however the more creatures you bond with, the weaker your bonds become since your loyalty becomes divided.
